
## How to Play
Controls:

Move Right: Right Arrow

Move Left: Left Arrow

Jump: Spacebar

- Mechanics:

The faster the player runs, the higher the jump (Run-Jump Boost).

Every few seconds, another block at the bottom of the tower falls and disappears.
