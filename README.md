## Game Description

The game is a demo inspired by Ice Tower, where the player climbs a series of blocks while the tower collapses beneath them. The goal is to survive as long as possible and reach the finish line before the floor disappears. Throughout the game, special items appear that grant the player temporary abilities-such as stronger or weaker jumps-that influence gameplay style and strategy.

The game combines fast movement, quick reactions, and dealing with constantly falling platforms, requiring the player to be agile and precise with their jumps.

## How to Play
Controls:

Move Right: Right Arrow

Move Left: Left Arrow

Jump: Spacebar

- Mechanics:

The faster the player runs, the higher the jump (Run-Jump Boost).

Every few seconds, another block at the bottom of the tower falls and disappears.
